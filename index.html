Our Story

The history of the Air Jordans begins in 1984, during a time when Nike was a struggling shoe company. At that time, Nike was known as a running shoe company and their running shoe phenomenon was slowly dying. Looking for a way to reinvent themselves and appeal to a new segment of the market, Nike turned to an NBA rookie named Michael Jordan. Michael was already endorsing several other products, but Nike felt that Michael could help them turn things around. Michael Jordan was hoping to sign with Adidas or Converse since he always loved Adidas and Converse were the shoes that he wore as a basketball player in North Carolina. Converse was not interested since they already had endorsement deals with Larry Bird and Magic Johnson . Adidas was not interested either.
